AgriComm Protocol

Enabling seamless communication between autonomous farm vehicles.

What is AgriComm?

AgriComm (Agricultural Communication Protocol) allows tractors, harvesters, drones, and irrigation robots to communicate and coordinate operations.

Why AgriComm?

  • Real-time synchronization between vehicles
  • Task delegation based on capacity
  • Secure multi-modal data sharing
  • Fleet-wide awareness and optimization
